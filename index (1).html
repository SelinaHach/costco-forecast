Bottom Line

Costco's profit is a margin story, not a revenue story. The ~200bps spread between Merchandise Margin (11.12%) and SG&A (9.25%), applied to $270B+ in revenue, generates $10B+ in operating income. Membership fees — only 1.9% of revenue — contribute ~51% of operating income at near-zero cost. Protect the margin spread. Watch membership quality. Revenue growth follows.
